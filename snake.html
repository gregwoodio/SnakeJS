How to play:

Use the arrow keys (or WASD) to move your snake around the area. Eat apples to get points, but don't crash into the walls or yourself.
  • Red squares: Red squares are apples. They give you one point and make the snake longer.
  • Yellow squares: Yellow squares are super apples, they give you five points and make the snake longer, but only stay in one spot for a short time.
  • Orange and Blue squares: Portals! Go in one side and come out the other. Be careful, you keep going the same direction so don't go into the portal too close to a wall.
